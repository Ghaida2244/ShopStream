ShopStream is a retail data engineering pipeline.
The pipeline receives online retail transactions through Apache Kafka.
Each transaction contains an invoice number, stock code, description, quantity, invoice date, unit price, customer ID, and country.
Pydantic validates incoming transactions. Invalid records are stored in a quarantine area.
Valid records are stored in the Bronze layer using Delta Lake.
The Silver layer cleans the records, calculates the total amount, removes duplicates, and applies merge operations.
The Gold layer aggregates retail data by country.
Great Expectations checks data quality in the Silver layer.
OpenLineage records pipeline lineage events.
Apache Airflow orchestrates the Silver transformation, data quality check, and Gold aggregation tasks.
